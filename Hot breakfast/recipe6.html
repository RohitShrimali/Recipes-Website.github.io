HOT BREAKFAST

Sprouted Matki Poha

Looking for some snazzy ways to top up on iron? Here’s a nutritious breakfast that will knock the tiredness out of you. The interesting combination of ingredients in the Sprouted Matki Poha makes it a power packed breakfast loaded with iron!The vitamin C in matki poha recipe further enhances the absorption of iron.Enjoy it hot!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup boiled sprouted matki (moath beans)
  • 2 cups thick beaten rice (jada poha)
  • 2 tsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  • 1 tsp finely chopped green chillies
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder (haldi)
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp chilli powder
  • salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped coriander (dhania)
  • 2 tsp lemon juice

METHOD

  1. To make sprouted matki poha, place the beaten rice in a sieve and hold it under running water for a few seconds. Keep aside for 2 minutes for the water to drain out completely.
  2. Heat the oil in a broad non-stick pan and add the cumin seeds.
  3. When the seeds crackle, add the green chillies and sauté on a medium flame for a few seconds.
  4. Add the onions and turmeric powder and sauté on a medium flame for 1 minute.
  5. Add the matki, soaked beaten rice, sugar, chilli powder, salt and coriander, mix well and cook on a medium flame for 2 to 3 minutes, while stirring occasionally.
  6. Add the lemon juice and mix well.
  7. Serve the sprouted matki poha hot.
