Moong Dal and Cauliflower
Greens Appe

Ever thought of using cauliflower greens to make a mouth-watering snack? Well, here is a unique Indian moong dal appe recipe that uses yellow moong dal and cauliflower greens to make sumptuous appe.Cauliflower greens are a rich source of iron, which helps you stay energetic all day long.Although very easy to make, this recipe requires a little pre-planning of soaking the dal.Make the appe just before serving, as they stiffen over time.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup yellow moong dal(split yellow gram)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped cauliflower greens
  • 1 tsp green chilli paste
  • 1 tsp roasted cumin seeds (jeera)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tsp oil for greasing

For Serving

Green Chutney

METHOD

  1. Clean,wash and soak the yellow moong dal in enough water for 3 to 4 hours.
  2. Drain and blend the moong dal in a mixer to a smooth paste using 1/4cup of water.
  3. Transfer the mixture into a deep bowl, add the cauliflower greens, greens chilli paste, cumin seeds and salt, mix well and keep aside.
  4. Heat the appe pan on a medium flame and grease it with 1 tsp of oil.
  5. Pour 1 tbsp of the batter into each mould, cover with a lid and cook on a medium flame till the outer surface becomes golden brown and then turn each appe upside down using a fork so as to cook on the other side. You can cook 6 appes at atime in 6 moulds.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 to make 6 more appes.
  7. Serve immediately with green chutney.
