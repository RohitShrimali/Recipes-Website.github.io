Kale Masoor Veg Antioxidant Healthy Office Salad

Trendy lunch? Look no further than a lunch salad . Salads are healthy, tasty, awesomely attractive and will make you shine with good health.Kale is bursting with the goodness of beta carotene, vitamins K and C , lutein, zeaxanthin and iron . Feta cheese and masoor are loaded with proteins , while vitamin rich capsicum promotes the formation of collagen and improves skin health. Bean sprouts contribute fibre to the recipe while carrots are good for your eyes. A wholesome and holistically healthy salad, this is really a must-try! When making a lunch salad, always pack the dressing separately and toss the two together just before eating.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/4 cup soaked and cooked masoor (whole red lentil)
  • 1/2 cup kale leaves , torn into pieces
  • 1/2 cup coloured capsicum cubes
  • 1/4 cup chopped mushrooms (khumbh)
  • 1/4 cup cucumber cubes
  • 1/4 cup red cabbage cubes
  • 1/4 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/4 cup sliced carrot
  • 2 tbsp carrot juliennes
  • 2 tbsp chopped spring onions whites and greens
  • 1 tbsp crumbled feta cheese
  • To Be Mixed Into A Dressing
  • 1/4 tsp grated garlic (lehsun)
  • 1/2 tsp chopped onions
  • 1/4 tsp dried mixed herbs
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp honey
  • sea salt (khada namak) to taste

METHOD

  1. It can be taken to work in a lunch box with a dressing in a separate small container.
  2. Just before eating, mix the dressing and toss well.
