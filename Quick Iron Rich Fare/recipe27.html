Spinach and Mint Juice

Put the excitement back into your day with this punchpacked Spinach and Mint Juice.Full of vitamin A and iron, this Palak Pudina Drink is also reinforced with lemon juice, which not only imparts a nice tanginess but also improves the absorption of iron due to the presence of vitamin C in it.Coriander and jal jeera powder lend a masaledar touch making this Spinach and Mint Juice all the more aromatic and flavourful. This Green Drink is a perfect antioxidant dose for mornings.

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 cups roughly chopped spinach (palak)
  • 1 cup finely chopped mint leaves (phudina)
  • 1/2 cup roughly chopped coriander (dhania)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp jal jeera powder , optional
  • crushed ice for serving

METHOD

  1. To make spinach and mint juice, combine the spinach, mint leaves and coriander along with Â½ cup of water in a juicer and blend till smooth.
  2. Strain the juice using a strainer.
  3. Add the lemon juice and jal jeera powder and mix well.
  4. Add some crushed ice in 4 small individual glasses and pour equal quantities of the juice over it.
  5. Serve the spinach and mint juice immediately.
