Buckwheat Dosa

buckwheat dosa known as kuttu dosa is an instant buckwheat dosa which requires no fermentation. Here is an easy and healthy Indian buckwheat crepes prepared with buckwheat and urad dal. The twist here is that the ingredients are powdered, tempered and then mixed into a batter, which can be cooked immediately into yummy buckwheat dosas.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup buckwheat (kuttu or kutti no daro)
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (split black lentils)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds ( rai / sarson)
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tsp finely chopped green chillies
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped coriander (dhania)
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 tsp oil for greasing
  • 10 tsp oil for cooking

METHOD

  1. To make buckwheat dosas, combine the buckwheat and urad dal in a mixer and blend to a fine powder.
  2. Transfer the powder in a deep bowl and keep aside.
  3. Heat the oil in a small pan and add the mustard seeds.
  4. When the seeds crackle, add the asafoetida and sauté on a medium flame for a few seconds.
  5. Add the tempering, green chillies, coriander, salt and approximately 3 ¼ cups of water and mix well.
  6. Heat a non-stick tava (griddle) and grease it lightly with oil.
  7. Pour a ladleful of batter in a circular manner. Pour a little oil in the holes of the buckwheat dosa and cook on both the sides till golden brown in colour.
  8. Repeat step 7 to make 19 more buckwheat dosas.
  9. Serve the buckwheat dosas immediately with green chutney.
