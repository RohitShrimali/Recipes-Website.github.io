BASIC RECIPE

Homemade Almond Milk

This is an instant almond milk which does not require and soaking of Almonds and then straining the blended milk. It has a pleasant taste, which is further enhanced by vanilla essence. Whatâ€™s more to perceive is that this homemade almond milk is free of preservatives and sugar, while being more economical at the same time.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup almonds (badam)
  • 4 cups cold water
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence

METHOD

  1. To make indian almond milk, combine all the ingredients in a mixer and blend till smooth. We recommend a high quality blender like a bullet blender / vitamix. If the quality of the blender is not good, the milk will not be smooth and there will be small almond pieces left.
  2. Refrigerate and serve chilled. It stays fresh for 3 days.
