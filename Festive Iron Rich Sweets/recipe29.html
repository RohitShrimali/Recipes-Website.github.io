Jowar and Pear Appam

The all-time favourite South Indian snack appam features here with a healthy twist, Jowar and Pear Appam recipe. We have combined two wholesome flours with grated pear to make a naturally-sweet batter. Laced with cardamom and honey and cooked with ghee in an appe mould, the Jowar and Pear Appam has a very rich taste that everyone will love. The grated fruit also gives the appam a soft and spongy texture.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup jowar (white millet) flour
  • 1/2 cup grated pears
  • 1/4 cup whole wheat flour (gehun ka atta)
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom (elaichi) powder
  • a pinch of salt
  • 2 1/4 tsp melted ghee for greasing and cooking

METHOD

  1. To make jowar and pear appam, combine all the ingredients along with approx. ¾ cup of water in a deep bowl and mix well.
  2. Grease an appe mould with ¼ tsp of ghee and put 1 tbsp of the batter into each of the 7 appe moulds.
  3. Cook using ½ tsp of ghee till the lower surface becomes golden brown in colour, then turn each appam upside down using a fork and cook them till they turn golden brown in colour from the other side.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 to make 14 more jowar and pear appams in 2 more batches.
  5. Serve the jowar and pear appam immediately.
