FESTIVE IRON RICH SWEETS

Mixed Til Chikki

Shiny white teeth and robust strong bones need a daily dose of calcium, and what better way to provide this essential nutrient than in the form of a crunchy Mixed Til Chikki! The ingredients of this chikki such as sesame seeds, almonds and jaggery, are all Calcium-rich foods, which help you avoid and overcome calcium deficiency

INGREDIENTS

  • 5 tbsp black sesame seeds (kala til)
  • 5 tbsp white sesame seeds (til)
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced almonds (badam)
  • 1 tsp ghee
  • 1/2 cup roughly chopped jaggery (gur)

METHOD

  1. Heat a broad non-stick pan, add both the sesame seeds and dry roast on a slow flame for a few minutes. Keep aside.
  2. Heat a small non-stick pan, add the almonds and dry roast on a medium flame for 2 minutes. Keep aside.
  3. Heat the ghee in a broad non-stick pan, add the jaggery, mix well and cook on a slow flame for 2 minutes, while stirring continuously.
  4. Remove from the flame, add the roasted sesame seeds and almonds and mix very well.
  5. Immediately transfer the mixture on the back side of a greased thali or a smooth greased stone surface. Roll it out into a 250 mm. (10”) diameter thin circle using a greased rolling pin.
  6. Cut it into 10 mm. X 10 mm. (½’’×½’’) 12 square pieces using a sharp knife.
  7. Allow them to cool completely.
  8. Serve or store in an air-tight container till use.
