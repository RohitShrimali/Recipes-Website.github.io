Badam ka Sheera

How many times have you dismissed the thought of making your favourite Indian desserts at home because they take too long to prepare? Well, where there is a will, there is a way. Next time you feel like relishing some delicious badam ka sheera, try this quick and easy recipe, which enables you to make this fabulous saffron-tinged, rich almond sheera in minutes!

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups almonds (badam)
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3 tbsp ghee
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom (elaichi) powder

METHOD

  1. To make badam ka sheera, heat enough water in a deep non-stick pan, add the almonds and boil on a high flame for 3 to 4 minutes.
  2. Switch off the flame, keep it aside for 5 minutes.
  3. Drain, peel the almonds.
  4. Dry them compeltely in a kitchen towel or a muslin cloth.
  5. Blend in a small mixer jar till coarse in 2 batches. Keep aside.
  6. Heat the ghee in a deep non-stick pan
  7. Add the coarsely crushed almond, mix well and cook on a medium flame for 3 to 4 minutes, while stirring continuously.
  8. Add the milk, mix well and cook on a medium flame for 1 to 2 minutes, while stirring continuously.
  9. Add the sugar, mix well and cook on a medium flame for 3 to 4 minutes, while stirring continuously.
  10. Add the cardamom powder and mix well.
  11. Serve the badam ka sheera hot or warm.
